SearchBlox Receives Recognition as KMWorld Trend-Setting Product of 2015

Share This Press Release

Making it easy to search and analyze enterprise-scale collections of unstructured data, SearchBlox stands at leading edge of industry, SearchBlox Software reports

Glen Allen, VA, United States of America - January 18, 2016 /MarketersMedia/ — SearchBlox Software, Inc., announced that the company's flagship product has been selected as a "Trend-Setting Product of 2015" by KMWorld, a leading authority on content, document, and knowledge management. Automatically drawing rich, rewarding insights and trends from unstructured data like Employee Surveys and Market Research reports, SearchBlox version 8.2 was recognized by KMWorld as a powerful tool for "delivering the right information to the right people at the right time." Since 2003, SearchBlox Software has been a leader in the field of enterprise search and analytics solutions, and the release of version 8.3 of SearchBlox since the granting of the KMWorld award has made the product even more capable.

"We're proud to report that KMWorld has highlighted SearchBlox as a true trend-setter in the field of knowledge management," SearchBlox Software co-founder Timo Selvaraj said, "With over 200 vendors having been surveyed and more than 1,000 different products considered, it is truly gratifying to receive this kind of recognition from one of the leading authorities in the industry. Even with SearchBlox attracting such notice, we are always looking for ways to make it even more capable, as our recent release of version 8.3 has shown."

With ever-expanding operational scales and huge stores of data that grow by the second, modern enterprises can seem almost unfathomable. This is true even with regard to structured, numeric data like financial figures and employee headcounts, and the challenges become even greater when far fuzzier qualitative reports and inherently subjective factors like consumer sentiments must be indexed and analyzed.

For over a decade, SearchBlox Software has been providing powerful, effective tools that ably tackle unstructured data stores at enterprise scales, condensing, analyzing, and summarizing them in ways that give decision makers and others exactly the information they need. SearchBlox is regularly used today, for example, to quickly, effectively identify important themes and trends from thousands of employee surveys at once, including the automatic creation of telling associations with demographic traits and roles.

The same powerful system can be used to aggregate and analyze text and document collections of any kind, allowing for the production of valuable insights from data stores that would otherwise be far too large to profitably plumb. From better understanding market research and employee feedback to enabling more effective enterprise-scale search of documents held organization-wide, SearchBlox gives customers the ability to index and analyze the most common form of data of all.

With over 300 companies spread across 30 countries now relying on SearchBlox, the influential product makes for a fitting recipient of KMWorld's Trend-Setting Products of 2015 award. Regularly updated with new features and performance improvements that make it even more capable, SearchBlox is described in full at the SearchBlox Software website at www.SearchBlox.com, where those interested can also sign up for a 30-day, risk-free trial.

About SearchBlox Software, Inc.:
Providing powerful, enterprise-oriented search and analytics solutions for unstructured data since 2003, SearchBlox Software is a leading force in the knowledge management industry with over 300 customers in 30 countries.
