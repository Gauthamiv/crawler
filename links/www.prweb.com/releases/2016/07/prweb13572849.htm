SearchBlox Enterprise Search and Text Analytics Solution Now Available as Cloud Service

Share Article

Cloud Search Service simplifies migration for Google Search Appliance (GSA) customers

Google Search Appliance (GSA) Migration Process

Combined with 24x7 support, SearchBlox is a cost-efficient and quickly deployed multilingual replacement solution for the Google Search Appliance (GSA). What used to take weeks and months of deployment and integration now takes minutes to get running.

Richmond, VA (PRWEB) July 26, 2016

SearchBlox Software, Inc., a leading provider of enterprise search and text analytics solutions based on Apache Lucene, has announced that its award-winning search and analytics product is now available as a cloud service.

SearchBlox continually aggregates and indexes data in real-time for search and analysis, as data is added via over 60 in-built data connectors, whether from websites, file systems social media, email, cloud storage, Salesforce, or other sources. In addition, the new cloud search service allows customers to start small and scale up as and when required, without a huge upfront investment.

With Google declaring end of life for the Google Search Appliance (GSA), SearchBloxâ€™s new cloud search service along with its fully-managed GSA Migration services minimizes risks for current GSA customers looking for a new replacement solution.    

"We have hosted and supported the search infrastructure for several clients over the last few years. This new cloud search service takes that capability to the next level by greatly simplifying the customer effort required to use SearchBlox's world-class search functionality," said Timo Selvaraj, Co-Founder and VP of Product Management at SearchBlox. "Combined with 24x7 support, SearchBlox is a cost-efficient and quickly deployed multilingual replacement solution for the Google Search Appliance (GSA). What used to take weeks and months of deployment and integration now takes minutes to get running."

About SearchBlox

SearchBlox is a leading provider of enterprise search solutions based on Apache Lucene. More than 350 customers in 30 countries use SearchBlox to power their websites, intranets and custom search applications. Since 2003, SearchBlox has been providing businesses with simple, affordable solutions for their data management needs including web-based administration and integrated data connectors to index enterprise and web content. In 2015, SearchBlox won recognition from KMWorld as a trendsetting product, and we are proud to stand out in the marketplace for enterprise search and text analytics. SearchBlox Software, Inc., is privately held and is based in Richmond, Virginia, USA. For more information, visit http://www.searchblox.com.
