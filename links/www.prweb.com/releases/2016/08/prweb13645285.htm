SearchBlox Enterprise Search and Text Analytics Named KMWorld Trend-Setting Product of 2016

Share Article

SearchBlox offers a guaranteed and risk free migration path for Google Search Appliance and Google Mini customers.

SearchBlox is KMWorld Trend-Setting Product of 2016

SearchBlox offers the fastest, easiest and risk-free option to replace your Google Search Appliance or Google Mini.

Richmond, VA (PRWEB) August 31, 2016

SearchBlox Software, a leading provider of enterprise search, sentiment and text analytics solutions, recently announced that SearchBlox’s version 8.4 has been chosen as one of KMWorld’s Trend-Setting Products of 2016. SearchBlox provides the best solution for migration away from the now-defunct Google Search Appliance and has already moved more than 50 customers since Google Mini and GSA were discontinued.

KMWorld Editor Sandra Haimila says, “Each year we look for software products that break new territory in helping organizations achieve their knowledge management goals. These products are designed, developed and perfectly timed to fill a niche in the marketplace before users are even aware that such functionality and flexibility have been missing. Designated as the KMWorld Trend-Setting Products of 2016, these offerings hold the promise for the marketplace.”

“Once again, we are delighted to have been selected by KMWorld as a Trend-Setting Product,” says Timo Selvaraj, SearchBlox Co-Founder and VP Product Management. “We are continually looking for new ways to stand out in the marketplace, and this year is no different. We maintain an unwavering commitment to providing best-in-class service to our customers, as well as offering a product that is continually evolving to provide the most up-to-date search technology, with flexibility, ease-of-use, and affordability.”

Haimila adds, “Our judging panel agrees that such characteristics as usefulness, scalability and level of innovation are among the most valuable components that we consider when we annually select the KMWorld Trend-Setting Products. And the panel believes that timing is key. Too late in the market and competitors already have a foothold. Too early and customers are nowhere to be found.”

In early 2016, SearchBlox was included in KMWorld’s 2016 list of 100 Companies that Matter in Knowledge Management, and was also awarded the Trend-Setting Product designation in 2015. Based in Richmond, Virginia, SearchBlox is considered a marketplace leader for enterprise search technologies, and proudly serves more than 350 customers in 30 countries.

About KMWorld
KMWorld (http://www.kmworld.com) is the leading information provider serving the Knowledge Management systems market and covers the latest in Content, Document and Knowledge Management, informing more than 30,000 subscribers about the components and processes - and subsequent success stories - that together offer solutions for improving business performance.

KMWorld is a publishing unit of Information Today, Inc. (http://www.infotoday.com)

About SearchBlox
Since 2003, SearchBlox has been providing businesses with simple, affordable solutions for their data management needs including web-based administration and integrated data connectors to index enterprise and web content. In 2015, SearchBlox won recognition from KMWorld as a trendsetting product, and we are proud to stand out in the marketplace for enterprise search and text analytics. SearchBlox Software, Inc., serves more than 350 customers in 30 countries, and is based in Richmond, Virginia, USA. For more information, visit http://www.searchblox.com.
