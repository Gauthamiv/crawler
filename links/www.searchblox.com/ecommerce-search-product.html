eCommerce Search that helps you sell more.

Embed an eCommerce Search anywhere to search for your products.

Your customers are searching for your products or services. SearchBlox will help the find them on your site.

Faceted Search for Products
SearchBlox provides a faceted search results plugin that can help you get started very quickly. Users are able to filter their search results through the facet filters, which are created dynamically on any field that you choose, including custom meta fields.

Mobile Search

Data Sync
Search results UI is website, tablet and mobile friendly through responsive design, and are compatible with Apple or Android apps. 
SearchBlox offers the ability to set up continuous sync for the product data files so they can always updated.

Autosuggest
SearchBlox provides autosuggest to provide typeahead suggestions on products available within the catalog.

Analytics
​The advanced real-time analytics in SearchBlox helps you understand what your users are looking for.
