Site Search with relevance and speed.

Embed a search box anywhere and enable your users to find the right webpages.

Signup and kick off the website crawler to see how SearchBlox enables search on your website.

Web Crawler
SearchBlox allows crawling of one or many websites. Administrators can specify include and exclude rules for the crawler and the type of files to make searchable. Both on-demand and scheduled crawls are supported. 

Search Results UI
  • Extensive options for your branded search results.
  • Ready-to-use simple, advanced and faceted search results page UI.
  • Embed as a widget on any webpage.

Fast & Contextual Faceted Search
  • Power searches from thousands of users with the fastest response times and automated machine learning.
  • Faceted search filters, search-term highlighting, keyword-in-context, synonyms, autosuggest and spelling suggestions are out of the box.

Automatic Metadata Indexing 

Real-Time Analytics
  • Automatic indexing of all HTML meta tags during crawling to enable searches for metadata search and faceted search filtering. 
  • ​​Understand what users are looking for and clicking on  with weekly, daily and hourly analytics.

Multilingual Support 
  • SearchBlox supports indexing of your website in 37 languages
  • Search is supported across collections containing content in different languages
  • Synonyms and autosuggest support

Featured Results
  • Sponsor or feature specific web content for search terms
  • Control when and how many times each featured result is displayed
  • Featured results are delivered as part of the search results and can be customized for look and feel
