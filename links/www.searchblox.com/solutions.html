SearchBlox Enterprise Search

​SearchBlox provides a robust and agile search and text analytics platform that can aggregate and search unstructured data from websites, file folders, feeds, cloud storage, email archives, databases, csv, and social streams like Twitter. The data is available for search and text analysis allowing businesses to gain insights into customers and products.

SearchBlox Professional Services

​Enrich, extract, and extrapolate text analytics to build powerful visualizations with SearchBlox’s search and text analytics platform. Drive value to your existing unstructured data in market research, brand marketing and customer engagement and let SearchBlox help you build business models for the highest value gain from your customers.
